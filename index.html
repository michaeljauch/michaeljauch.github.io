Michael Jauch

About me

I'm a postdoctoral associate at the Center for Applied Mathematics at Cornell University working with David Matteson. I received my PhD from the Department of Statistical Science at Duke University where I was advised by Peter Hoff and David Dunson.

My research has ranged from applied work with scientific collaborators to theory and methods. I am particularly interested in multivariate data, time series, functional data, inference under shape or stochastic order constraints, random matrices, computational statistics, and statistical methods for single molecule experiments.

My CV can be found here. I can be reached by email at maj225@cornell.edu.
