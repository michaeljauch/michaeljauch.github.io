Michael Jauch

About me

I'm an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at Florida State University. My research interests include Bayesian statistics, multivariate data, computational methods, inference under shape or stochastic order constraints, and scientific applications.

Prior to arriving at Florida State, I was a Postdoctoral Associate at the Center for Applied Mathematics at Cornell University working with David Matteson. I received my PhD from the Department of Statistical Science at Duke University, where I was advised by Peter Hoff and David Dunson.

My (soon to be updated) CV can be found here. I can be reached by email at mjauch@fsu.edu.
